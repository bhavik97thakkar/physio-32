GRC Rakshitah

Your One-Stop Platform for Risk, Compliance, and Security Oversight

GRC Rakshitah by 9USRcraft simplifies the complex world of risk and compliance management with a unified platform that automates, monitors, and centralizes your entire InfoSec program

Designed for dynamic and compliance-intensive industries, it helps you build trust with customers, speed up audits, and reduce security risks through real-time visibility and intelligent automation

Detailed Overview

GRC Rakshitah empowers organizations to manage risk, ensure ongoing compliance, and maintain a strong security posture effortlessly.

From real-time risk tracking to seamless audit workflows and multi-framework compliance, our intelligent GRC tool enables teams to move fast without compromising control or governance.

How GRC Rakshitah Works

Assess

Run risk assessments and control mappings based on your cloud and business environment. Identify vulnerabilities, scope compliance needs, and map controls instantly. Get a complete inventory of assets including cloud services, SaaS platforms, data repositories, and access points.

Activate

Automate control monitoring and compliance checks. Set up alerts, reminders, and workflow triggers to keep your environment always audit-ready. Leverage integrations to collect real-time evidence and implement custom policies at scale

Attest

Generate audit-ready evidence, collaborate directly with auditors, and complete assessments quickly and smoothly using the Rakshitah dashboard. Support third-party attestations, certification renewals, and auditor documentation from one pane of glass
